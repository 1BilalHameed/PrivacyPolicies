Privacy Policy for Status Saver

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Status Saver ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using Status Saver, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Important: Status Saver is designed to work locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All operations are performed on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do NOT collect any personal information. Status Saver does not require registration, login, or any form of personal identification.

1.2 Media Files

The App accesses WhatsApp status files stored on your device's storage solely to display them within the application. These files remain on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

1.3 Usage Data

We do not collect any analytics, usage statistics, or behavioral data. The App functions entirely offline.

2. Permissions Required

Status Saver requires the following permissions to function properly:

2.1 Storage Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): Required to read WhatsApp status files from your device storage.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+): Required to access image files from WhatsApp statuses.
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13+): Required to access video files from WhatsApp statuses.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below): Required to save status files to your device's gallery.
  • MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 11+): Required to access WhatsApp's media folder in the new Android storage structure.

2.2 Internet Permission

  • INTERNET: Required solely for displaying advertisements through Google AdMob. No user data is transmitted over the internet by our app.
Note: All permissions are used exclusively for the core functionality of the app and are never used to collect or transmit personal data.

3. How We Use Information

Status Saver uses the accessed information only to:

  • Display WhatsApp status files (images and videos) within the app
  • Save status files to your device's gallery when you choose to download them
  • Maintain a local database of saved statuses on your device

We do not:

  • Upload any files to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties (except as noted in the Third-Party Services section)
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Collect any personal identifiable information

4. Data Storage

All data processed by Status Saver is stored locally on your device:

  • Saved Status Database: A local SQLite database stores references to files you've saved, including file paths and metadata. This database is stored in your app's private storage.
  • Preferences: App settings (such as premium status) are stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences.
  • Downloaded Files: Status files you save are stored in your device's DCIM/StatusSaver folder.

No data leaves your device except for standard Google AdMob ad requests (see Third-Party Services section).

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google AdMob

Status Saver uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • IP address
  • Advertising ID
  • App interaction data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy:

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Visiting Google's Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

Users who purchase the premium version of the app will have all advertisements removed. No ads-related data will be collected for premium users.

7. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device in secure, app-private storage
  • We use Android's built-in security features to protect your data
  • No user data is transmitted to external servers
  • The app does not have backend servers or cloud storage

However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data stored on your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Status Saver does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not target children and is intended for general audiences. Since we don't collect any personal information, we do not have data from children to delete. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app, you can simply uninstall it from their device.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete individual saved statuses within the app using the delete button
  • You can delete all app data by clearing app data in your device settings (Settings > Apps > Status Saver > Storage > Clear Data)
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all app data from your device
  • Downloaded status files in your gallery can be deleted manually from your device's file manager or gallery app
